Who I Am

I'm a creative and hardworking Junior at Lipscomb University. I have a uniquely diverse background in creative fields and technical areas, giving me a great combination of creative intuition and analytical problem-solving. In addition to my consistent drive to improve and learn more about the User Experience field, I highly value critical soft skills like effective communication, clear writing, and time management.

I'm currently pursuing a Web Development major with a focus on UI/UX Design, and I'm working towards a career in User Experience Design or Audio Experience Design.

Fun fact: I'm also an avid music composer! I love creating music in all sorts of different styles, and I also heartily enjoy learning piano.
